This file contains various words that have not been put into the TAGALOG
Files, because of any of the following reasons....

 * I do not class most of the words as Vital for a Beginner - The main
   Tagalog words should be good enough to start you off with.

 * I class most of the words as more aimed at Conversation than aimed
   at Beginners.

 * Some are just words that were not put into the main TAGALOG Files when I
   began writing LGUI and the TAGALOG Files.

 Note: Unless a word is marked Adj (Adjective), Vrb (Verb), Prn (Pronoun),
       Adv (Adverb) or Conj (Conjunction) it is to be taken as a Noun.
